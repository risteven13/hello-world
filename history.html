Why Luke started Hunt's Hens:

I could never find eggs that met the standards that I longed for,
eggs that come from free range, cage free, non GMO, organic, herb fed chickens.
I decided that I would raise chickens of my own AND
meet all of those standards along with a fancy, custom, hand-stamped egg carton.
Our chickens are kept healthy with over 20 varieties of garden grown herbs including
oregano, mint, sage, lemon balm, bee balm, lavender, rosemary,
thyme, basil, parsley, dill, catnip, peppermint, and multiple varieties of some.

- Luke
